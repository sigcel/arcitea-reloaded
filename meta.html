History

Arcitea was a guide made by a user of the bodybuilding forums, Joseph Arcita I believe, as can be seen by the large amounts of links to specific threads. It was never properly advertised on the forums however, and the owner never made his own thread announcing its creation. The original creator also made a relatively popular blogspot concerning the keto diet, which is still around, you can find it here.

The site was mentioned on the /sig/ wiki but was bascially forgotten when the site died. In August, 2018, Arcitea Rede/sig/ned was created but stopped receiving updates in October, 2018.

It was a good guide though, so it is being reborn, redesigned, and reloaded.
