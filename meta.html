What is this?

History

Arcitea was a guide originally written by a user on bodybuilding forums, Joseph Arcita. It was never properly advertised on the forums however, and the owner never made his own thread announcing its creation. The owner also made a relatively popular blog concerning the keto diet, which is still around, you can find it here.

The site was mentioned on the /sig/ wiki but was bascially forgotten when the site died. In August, 2018, Arcitea Rede/sig/ned was created, but never completed, and stopped receiving updates in October, 2018.

It was a good guide though, so it is being reborn, redesigned, and reloaded.

Keep in mind this site has gone through various iterations and is a collective effort, so writing styles and references to personal life may differ depending on who wrote what section.
