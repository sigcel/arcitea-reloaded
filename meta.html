History

This website was originally a singular post found on Arcitea. The website died and was revived by a /sig/ user with Arcitea Rede/sig/ned, but it has lacked updates since late 2018. The guide was originally written by a user of the bodybuilding forums, Joseph Arcita. Arcitea was never properly advertised on the forums, and was essentially forgotten when the /sig/ wiki (which mentioned the website) died. In August, 2018, Arcitea Rede/sig/ned was created, but never completed, and stopped receiving updates in October, 2018.

Keep in mind this website has gone through various iterations and is a collective effort, so writing styles and references to personal life may differ depending on who wrote what section.
